About the Book

Join Bambam, a lovable but nervous dog, on his first sailing adventure! This heartwarming story follows Bambam as he overcomes his fears and discovers the joy of sailing on the San Francisco Bay.

Through gentle storytelling and charming illustrations, young readers will learn about courage, friendship, and the excitement of trying new things. Perfect for children ages 3-7, this book will inspire both young sailors and animal lovers alike.
