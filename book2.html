Sail Away with Me

A playful introduction to sailing for young children

About the Book

"Sail Away with Me" is a delightful picture book that introduces young children to the joy of sailing through the eyes of a curious young sailor. With vibrant illustrations and engaging text, this book makes learning about sailing fun and accessible for children ages 3-7.

Follow along as our young protagonist learns about different types of boats, basic sailing terms, and the importance of safety on the water. The story is filled with gentle humor and educational content that will spark children's interest in sailing and marine life.

Each page features detailed illustrations of the San Francisco Bay, complete with iconic landmarks and local marine wildlife. The book includes a glossary of sailing terms and fun facts about boats and the ocean, making it both entertaining and educational.
