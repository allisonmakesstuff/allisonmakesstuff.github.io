A Day on the Bay

A baby's introduction to the magic of sailing on the San Francisco Bay

About the Book

Inspire dreams of sailing with the wildlife around San Francisco with this memorable counting book. Through a simple counting pattern and fun animal illustrations, young readers will discover the wonders of boats, the bay, and marine life. This charming board book introduces basic sailing concepts in a way that's perfect for babies and toddlers.

With bright, friendly illustrations and gentle rhyming text, "A Day on the Bay" creates a perfect first exposure to sailing that will spark curiosity and wonder in even the youngest readers. Each page features simple, engaging scenes of boats, sea creatures, and the beautiful San Francisco Bay.

This book is designed to be durable and safe for little hands, with rounded corners and sturdy pages that can withstand enthusiastic reading sessions. It's the perfect gift for sailing families or anyone who wants to introduce their little ones to the magic of the sea, and a wonderful keepsake for reminiscing about a trip to San Francisco!
