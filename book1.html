The Last Horizon

A tale of adventure and discovery across the Pacific

About the Book

In "The Last Horizon," readers are taken on an unforgettable journey across the vast Pacific Ocean. The story follows Sarah Mitchell, a seasoned sailor who embarks on a solo voyage from San Francisco to Hawaii, seeking answers to questions that have haunted her for years.

As she navigates through treacherous waters and faces the challenges of isolation, Sarah discovers that the greatest journey is the one within. The novel masterfully weaves together elements of adventure, self-discovery, and the raw beauty of the open sea.

Drawing from real sailing experiences and maritime knowledge, the author creates an authentic and immersive world that will captivate both sailing enthusiasts and general readers alike.
