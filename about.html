About the Author

The journey behind the stories

With over two decades of sailing experience across the world's most challenging waters, our author brings a unique perspective to maritime literature. Having logged more than 50,000 nautical miles, including solo crossings of the Atlantic and Pacific oceans, the stories penned are deeply rooted in real experiences and authentic sailing knowledge.

The journey into writing began after a particularly challenging solo voyage from San Francisco to Hawaii, where the isolation and beauty of the open ocean inspired the first novel, "The Last Horizon." This debut work quickly gained recognition in the sailing community for its authentic portrayal of life at sea and the human spirit's resilience.

The second book, "Wind's Whisper," draws from years of teaching sailing and mentoring new sailors. It explores the philosophical aspects of sailing and how the lessons learned at sea can be applied to life on land. The book has been praised for its ability to connect with both experienced sailors and those who have never set foot on a boat.

When not writing or sailing, the author conducts workshops and seminars on maritime safety and navigation, contributing to the sailing community's knowledge base. The goal is to inspire a new generation of sailors and readers to explore the world's oceans while respecting their power and beauty.
